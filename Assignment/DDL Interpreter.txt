DDL Interpreter

A DDL (Data Definition Language) interpreter is a module in a database management system (DBMS) that processes and executes Data Definition Language statements.

DDL statements are responsible for creating, altering, and deleting database objects such as tables, indexes, and views.

The DDL interpreter in a DBMS performs the following key functions:
1.Parsing
2.Validation
3.Execution
4.Metadata Management
